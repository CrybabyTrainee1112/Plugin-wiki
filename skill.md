# Combo Skills — Optimized Combos Per Power

This document provides a concise, optimized combo for each power implemented in the project. Each entry includes role, recommended triggers, an example combat sequence, strengths, weaknesses, and tuning suggestions.

> Test combos on a staging server. Balance values (cooldowns, radii, caps) should be tuned per-server.

---

## How to read each entry
- Role: primary tactical use of the power.
- Recommended triggers: suggested binds or actions.
- Sequence: short ideal order to use abilities in a fight.
- Strengths / Weaknesses: what the combo excels at and its counters.
- Tuning: quick config tips for admins.

---

## Per-Power Optimized Combos

### ICE
- Role: Crowd control and peel.
- Recommended triggers: Sneak / Left Click for slow/stun abilities.
- Sequence: Apply area slow → create ice barrier to separate → reposition or follow-up with single-target burst.
- Strengths: Strong disengage and kite control.
- Weaknesses: Low direct burst damage; vulnerable to ranged pressure.
- Tuning: Shorten freeze duration in PvP; keep cast time low for responsiveness.

### WARP
- Role: Blink mobility for engages and escapes.
- Recommended triggers: Right Click (instant warp) / Bind to quick slot.
- Sequence: Warp behind/around enemy → land quick attack/ability → Warp out or to safe portal.
- Strengths: Hard to pin down; enables flanks and saves.
- Weaknesses: Predictable escape routes; cooldown abuse if too short.
- Tuning: Add short invulnerability window and set 3–6s cooldown.

### LIGHTNING
- Role: High single-target burst and chain damage.
- Recommended triggers: Right Click for charged strike.
- Sequence: Use control (Ice/Gravity) to lock target → unleash Lightning for maximum effect.
- Strengths: Excellent burst finish, good against grouped targets if chained.
- Weaknesses: Needs setup for consistency; can overclear in small arenas.
- Tuning: Limit chain length and increase cooldown when chained to multiple players.

### UNSTABLE
- Role: Chaotic AoE with unpredictable effects (risk/reward).
- Recommended triggers: Active cast (any primary bind).
- Sequence: Open with Unstable to disrupt enemy positioning → capitalize on disarray with focus fire.
- Strengths: Great for chaos and breaking enemy formations.
- Weaknesses: Hard to control; may harm allies.
- Tuning: Add friendly-fire check and lower amplitude for PvP.

### SHOGUN
- Role: Melee burst and spacing control.
- Recommended triggers: Left Click combos / Sneak for charged strikes.
- Sequence: Close to melee range → use high-damage combos → retreat using ward or dash.
- Strengths: Strong duelist, high single-target output.
- Weaknesses: Predictable if telegraphed; weak at range.
- Tuning: Provide short recovery frames between heavy strikes.

### FIRE
- Role: Area denial and sustained DPS.
- Recommended triggers: Right Click for AoE; Sneak+Right Click to place zones.
- Sequence: Place fire zones → use Wind to push enemies into zones → reapply or retreat.
- Strengths: Great for zoning and objective denial.
- Weaknesses: Friendly fire, dependent on terrain.
- Tuning: Cap spread radius and add burn-DPS cap.

### WITCHER
- Role: Utility and anti-magic/monster control.
- Recommended triggers: Contextual (right click/use) on targets.
- Sequence: Use Witcher debuffs/support → coordinate with teammates to finish afflicted targets.
- Strengths: Strong against summoned/minion-heavy compositions.
- Weaknesses: Niche utility; lower raw damage.
- Tuning: Increase detection radius for summons and limit cooldown.

### NATURE
- Role: Sustain, roots, and battlefield control.
- Recommended triggers: Passive/periodic or Right Click for area heals.
- Sequence: Root or heal allies early → place plant obstacles → stall enemies while allies reposition.
- Strengths: Sustained healing and peel.
- Weaknesses: Relies on positioning and length of fight.
- Tuning: Limit heal per second and global range.

### PHOENIX
- Role: High AoE damage with self-sustain.
- Recommended triggers: Ultimate/charged cast.
- Sequence: Initiate with Phoenix to gain regen → follow with Fire zones to pressure enemies while recovering.
- Strengths: Self-healing enables risky plays.
- Weaknesses: Vulnerable during long cast windows.
- Tuning: Cap total heal per activation.

### NONE
- Role: No abilities.
- Recommended triggers: N/A
- Sequence: Play normally without power toolkit.
- Strengths/Weaknesses: N/A
- Tuning: N/A

### TWILIGHT_MIRAGE
- Role: Illusion, misdirection, and escape.
- Recommended triggers: Sneak/Right Click to create decoys.
- Sequence: Create mirage → bait enemy → flank or escape while they target illusions.
- Strengths: Excellent for outplays and confusion.
- Weaknesses: Low direct damage.
- Tuning: Limit number of active illusions and lifetime.

### ETERNITY
- Role: Time manipulation — brief slows or rewinds.
- Recommended triggers: Charged ability / Right Click.
- Sequence: Slow key enemy actions → force windows where allies can capitalize; use rewind defensively.
- Strengths: Strong utility for turning fights.
- Weaknesses: Complexity, potential for abuse.
- Tuning: Restrict radius and total time-manipulation per minute.

### CURSEWEAVER
- Role: Debuff stacking and DoT amplification.
- Recommended triggers: On-hit procs or Right Click curses.
- Sequence: Apply stacking curse → add DoT sources (Poison/Blood) to maximize damage over time.
- Strengths: Scales in long fights.
- Weaknesses: Weak early; needs setup.
- Tuning: Cap stacks and add diminishing returns.

### THUNDER_GOD
- Role: Enhanced lightning control with area disruptions.
- Recommended triggers: Ultimate / Right Click for heavy strikes.
- Sequence: Use Thunder God to interrupt and damage grouped enemies → follow with Wind/Lightning combos.
- Strengths: Powerful AoE interrupts.
- Weaknesses: High cooldown.
- Tuning: Increase cast-upfront telegraph to allow counterplay.

### WIND
- Role: Displacement and setup for AoE.
- Recommended triggers: Right Click or Sneak+Right Click to push.
- Sequence: Push or pull enemies into kill zones → combine with Fire or Spike to maximize outcome.
- Strengths: Crowd control and combo enabler.
- Weaknesses: Can push enemies out of friendly AoE.
- Tuning: Reduce push strength vs players in safe zones.

### DEMON
- Role: Debuff and pressure; strong in sustained trades.
- Recommended triggers: Active debuff cast / on-hit corruption.
- Sequence: Debuff priority targets → use Blood/Vampire for sustain while trading.
- Strengths: Great in prolonged encounters.
- Weaknesses: Falls off in quick skirmishes.
- Tuning: Limit stacking frequency and debuff potency.

### WATER
- Role: Slow, pull, and area control.
- Recommended triggers: Right Click for water fields / Sneak to channel.
- Sequence: Create water slow → combine with Earth to trap or with Lightning to punish immobilized targets.
- Strengths: Strong crowd control.
- Weaknesses: Ineffective on open maps with strong mobility.
- Tuning: Limit whirlpool radius and suction strength.

### WITHER
- Role: Damage over time and attrition.
- Recommended triggers: On-hit or active AoE application.
- Sequence: Spread Wither effect → focus targets over time while using cover and kiting.
- Strengths: Wears down tanky foes.
- Weaknesses: Low upfront burst; vulnerable to cleanses.
- Tuning: Reduce DoT tick rate in PvP.

### ICE_DRAGON
- Role: Ranged freeze and area denial.
- Recommended triggers: Charged ranged attacks.
- Sequence: Open from range to slow/freeze → switch to melee finishers (Snowparting Blade) when enemies are isolated.
- Strengths: Zoning and control at range.
- Weaknesses: Telegraphed projectiles.
- Tuning: Limit projectile count and cold stacking.

### WOOD_DRAGON
- Role: Nature control with sustain and area anchors.
- Recommended triggers: Place-nodes / Passive auras.
- Sequence: Use nodes to control movement → force enemies into killzones or traps.
- Strengths: Strong map control.
- Weaknesses: Weak 1v1 without team support.
- Tuning: Limit active node count per player.

### SNOWPARTING_BLADE
- Role: Assassin tool with freezing follow-up.
- Recommended triggers: Left Click combos / Sneak for charge.
- Sequence: Close gap → execute heavy strike → apply freezing follow-up from Ice-based companions.
- Strengths: Lethal single-target potential.
- Weaknesses: Melee risk; needs setup.
- Tuning: Add brief invulnerability frames on successful assassination.

### METEOR_LORD
- Role: Large AoE siege and heavy disruption.
- Recommended triggers: Ultimate call (long cooldown).
- Sequence: Mark impact zone → force enemies into or out of zone with Wind/Shockwave → detonate Meteor.
- Strengths: Game-changing AoE.
- Weaknesses: High telegraph; predictable counterplay.
- Tuning: Add cast delay and warning particles; limit per-minute uses.

### CULTIVATOR
- Role: Martial buffs and stacked damage output.
- Recommended triggers: Charge / Combo keystrokes.
- Sequence: Build-up buffs → unleash burst rotation while sustained by Nature or Blood.
- Strengths: Strong single-target sustained DPS.
- Weaknesses: Ramp-up time.
- Tuning: Balance buff duration and potency.

### HUASHAN
- Role: Agile martial artist with mobility and burst.
- Recommended triggers: Movement-based combos and special binds.
- Sequence: Use mobility to dodge → strike with high-precision combos → disengage.
- Strengths: Hard to hit; strong outplay potential.
- Weaknesses: More skill-dependent.
- Tuning: Limit combo length and cooldown on mobility moves.

### ALCOHOLIZM
- Role: Fun / situational buffs with randomness.
- Recommended triggers: Consume item / Active buff.
- Sequence: Activate for unpredictable buffs → rely on team discipline during randomness.
- Strengths: Entertaining and event-focused.
- Weaknesses: Not competitive for serious play.
- Tuning: Use only in minigames or with limited potency.

### POTATO
- Role: Novelty power for events.
- Recommended triggers: Active or passive laughs.
- Sequence: Use comedic effects to distract; avoid imbalance.
- Strengths: Party/minigame value.
- Weaknesses: Not for competitive balance.
- Tuning: Disable in ranked or PvP worlds.

### SUPERIOR_WARP
- Role: Long-range repositioning and strategic redeploy.
- Recommended triggers: Charged warp ability.
- Sequence: Use Superior Warp to escape or to instantly join flanks → follow with short-range attacks.
- Strengths: Excellent strategic mobility.
- Weaknesses: Long cooldown; map-dependent.
- Tuning: Limit uses per minute and add travel fade effect.

### DEMON_LORD
- Role: Heavy debuffing and frontline dominance.
- Recommended triggers: Ultimate active with area aura.
- Sequence: Enter frontlines → apply heavy corruption/debuffs → extend fight using Vampire/Blood sustain.
- Strengths: Terrifying in prolonged fights.
- Weaknesses: Large cooldown and telegraphed entry.
- Tuning: Cap area and debuff stacking.

### GRAVITY
- Role: Pull/push control and anti-air.
- Recommended triggers: Right Click to toggle area effect.
- Sequence: Use Gravity to group enemies or drop aerials → follow with Shockwave or Spike.
- Strengths: Excellent for controlling verticality.
- Weaknesses: May disrupt allies.
- Tuning: Add entity whitelist or toggle for friendly players.

### SHOCKWAVE
- Role: Close-range crowd separation and stun.
- Recommended triggers: Melee/Right Click near enemies.
- Sequence: Eject or stun frontline → follow up with AoE (Meteor/Fire) while enemies are displaced.
- Strengths: Breaks formations.
- Weaknesses: Short range; timing dependent.
- Tuning: Reduce knockback in confined areas.

### PORTAL
- Role: Tactical repositioning and team play.
- Recommended triggers: Place portal (Right Click) / Use portal to traverse.
- Sequence: Place portal behind objectives → coordinate team to funnel enemies into traps or escape routes.
- Strengths: Strategic value and team utility.
- Weaknesses: Abusable for spawn-peeking.
- Tuning: Limit active portals per player and add placement cooldown.

### CLOUD
- Role: Aerial movement and temporary high ground.
- Recommended triggers: Passive while active; Sneak to ride.
- Sequence: Gain height advantage with Cloud → use Gravity to force enemies down or snipe from above.
- Strengths: Map control and safe repositioning.
- Weaknesses: Vulnerable to anti-air counters.
- Tuning: Limit duration and cooldown between uses.

### VAMPIRE
- Role: Life-steal sustain in melee fights.
- Recommended triggers: On-hit passive / Active toggle.
- Sequence: Engage with sustain active → trade hits while recovering health; retreat when below threshold.
- Strengths: Great for attrition and 1v1 stamina.
- Weaknesses: Less effective vs ranged poke.
- Tuning: Cap percentage healed per hit and per second.

### POISON
- Role: Long-lasting DoT and area denial.
- Recommended triggers: Apply via projectiles or contact.
- Sequence: Poison targets then kite or trap them with Earth/Crystal.
- Strengths: Hard to cleanse in fast trades.
- Weaknesses: Slow damage curve; counters with cleanse.
- Tuning: Lower duration or stack potency limits in PvP.

### AIR
- Role: Light displacement and mobility boost.
- Recommended triggers: Short burst activations / Jump-based.
- Sequence: Use Air to reposition teammates or yourself → follow with Wind for extra displacement.
- Strengths: Mobility and minor CC.
- Weaknesses: Low direct damage.
- Tuning: Cap velocity gains near spawn.

### BLOOD
- Role: Aggressive sustain and bleed stacking.
- Recommended triggers: On-hit procs / Active drain.
- Sequence: Build Bleed stacks → activate drain to convert damage into healing.
- Strengths: Strong sustain in trades.
- Weaknesses: Requires multiple hits to be effective.
- Tuning: Cap drain conversion and stack duration.

### CRYSTAL
- Role: Defensive cover and choke creation.
- Recommended triggers: Place (Right Click) for barriers.
- Sequence: Place Crystal cover → funnel enemies into Spike/Earth traps or use as shield while allies rejoin.
- Strengths: Reliable cover and zoning.
- Weaknesses: Stationary; countered by ranged siege.
- Tuning: Limit number of simultaneous crystals and total health.

### EARTH
- Role: Barrier creation and terrain manipulation.
- Recommended triggers: Place/Right Click to raise walls.
- Sequence: Shape battlefield with Earth → block flanks and create chokepoints → layer with Spike.
- Strengths: Strong control over engagements.
- Weaknesses: Can be bypassed with flying/warp mobility.
- Tuning: Limit wall height and lifetime.

### MAGNETIC
- Role: Repositioning of metal entities/items and pulls.
- Recommended triggers: Active field toggle.
- Sequence: Pull enemies or items into traps → follow with Spike/Fire for damage.
- Strengths: Unique utility for environmental manipulation.
- Weaknesses: Limited vs non-metallic targets.
- Tuning: Add target filtering and cooldown per pull.

### DEATH
- Role: Execute and heavy single-target damage.
- Recommended triggers: Charged ultimate / On-kill procs.
- Sequence: Use Death to finish wounded targets → combine with Demon debuffs to lower thresholds.
- Strengths: High lethality.
- Weaknesses: Large cooldown and counters via shields.
- Tuning: Add fail-safe to avoid instant death in spawn zones.

### SOUND
- Role: Disruption, disorientation, and short stuns.
- Recommended triggers: Active pulses (Right Click).
- Sequence: Use Sound to interrupt channels → follow with Wind to reposition stunned targets.
- Strengths: Breaks enemy casts and coordination.
- Weaknesses: Limited damage.
- Tuning: Limit audible range in crowded servers.

### SPIKE
- Role: Lethal traps and area denial.
- Recommended triggers: Place traps on ground (Right Click).
- Sequence: Lay Spike traps in chokepoints → lure enemies using Wind/Gravity → detonate or let contact trigger.
- Strengths: Strong passive damage in controlled areas.
- Weaknesses: Accidental friendly kills.
- Tuning: Add friendly-player immunity and conditional triggers.

### LUNAR
- Role: Night/light based buffs and stealth.
- Recommended triggers: Passive at night / Active in shadows.
- Sequence: Activate Lunar for buffs → coordinate night assaults with stealth powers.
- Strengths: Powerful in night maps or dark areas.
- Weaknesses: Map/time dependent.
- Tuning: Scale potency with time-of-day settings.

### COMICTEST
- Role: Experimental; testing mechanics.
- Recommended triggers: Developer-defined.
- Sequence: Varies.
- Strengths/Weaknesses: N/A — use for testing only.
- Tuning: Keep moderate power and set to test worlds.
